I'm sharing a full remaster I just completed yesterday for the 2006-06-28 Shepherds Bush Empire gig. I at least got this finished and uploaded on the correct date, even though it is 2 years too early to be a true 20 year anniversary, so it is the ALMOST 20 year anniversary haha

It started from me wanting to do this remaster edit on Supermassive Black Hole since this has the single best performance of that song. I just decided to keep going with it once that song turned out the way it did. This is the way this gig was meant to heard. Please use decent headphones or put it on in the car to really experience how this sounds - playing on your phone likely won't do much.

I made these as 320kbps MP3s because there is absolutely no reason to bloat the files since the best source for the audio (only some songs, not even all of them) had a bit rate of 384kbps. There will be no noticeable difference/loss - they still sound amazing.

Only exception being track 6 into 7 where there was just nothing I could do there.

Grab it, turn it up, and please let me know what you think! I would welcome any feedback if it could have been done better - I think it came out ACE. I also recommend trying it with no EQ first since I make me edits to sound good without it.
